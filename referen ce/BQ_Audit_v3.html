Your Audit Report

Here's a summary of what we found in demo-project / analytics_123

48% score
Your data is not quite ready yet
⚠ Gaps Found — Action Needed

Your website tracking is working, but we don't yet have enough data about who your users are and what actions they take. This limits what AI tools can do for you right now.

🔍 What did we find?

We can't identify most of your visitors
Only 1 in 20 people visiting your site can be recognised when they come back. Think of it like a shop where almost everyone wears a mask — it's hard to give them a personalised experience or retarget them with ads.
Too few people are taking the action you care about
Less than 1 in 100 visitors are completing a purchase or key goal. AI models need more examples of "success" to learn what a good customer looks like — similar to training a new sales rep with too few real examples.
Limited history to spot seasonal patterns
We don't have enough past data to understand how your customers behave across different times of the year (e.g. peak sales seasons, holidays).
Your recent tracking looks healthy
Good news — your website is actively recording events and the data from the last 30 days is consistent. The infrastructure is in good shape.

✅ What should we do next?

Count smaller wins, not just final purchases
Instead of only tracking completed purchases, also track when someone views a product, adds it to their cart, or starts checkout. This gives AI models far more data to learn from — often 10 to 20 times more.
Encourage users to log in or register
When users are logged in, we can recognise them reliably. Even a simple "Save your cart" or newsletter sign-up prompt can significantly improve our ability to personalise their experience.
Re-run this audit in 4–6 weeks
Once these changes are live, run the audit again. Based on our estimates, your score should improve to 70%+ — enough to unlock the use cases below.
